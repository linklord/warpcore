## Mathjax

Inline equations can be used in between (eg, `$ f(x) = \frac{x}{(x + 1)^2} $`) and display as $ f(x) = \frac{x}{(x + 1)^2} $.

Block equations can also be used:

```
$$ e = mc^2 $$
```

and display as:

$$ e = mc^2 $$
